Design Challenge

Starting Questions and answers about DrugSense

What is the problem you’re trying to solve?

Ending the War on Drugs mentality, and shifting the focus to better help people who are suffering.

1) Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can the website prompt action from everyday people?

2) Now, state the ultimate impact you are trying to have.

increase awareness and garner support to help end the war on drugs

3) What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Align the visual focus to the ideological focus of the website - get rid of clutter. Navigate the user to do either action: learn more or join mailing petitions.

4) Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

Focus on an american audience, ease the user into relearning what was already taught about the war on drugs—this should focus news and petitions a bit better.

5) Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can this website prompt action from Americans looking to learn/ help the efforts against the war on drugs.
