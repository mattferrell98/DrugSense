Audience & Goals

An Exploration of Who The Audience Is and What Their Goals Are.

Audience

My sticky note progress ended up a bit different than the guidelines. I basically made a graph chart, where the horizontal axis is considering if the user needs convincing about the War on Drugs issue, while the vertical axis is considering whether the user will be the one giving help, or needing help. This process was landed on because of the nature of the audience. I noticed that many sections in the guidelines overlapped with eachother heavily, so doing it like this made more sense in context.

General Goals

Client

  • Raise awareness about the war on drugs issue.
  • Encourage more people to take action (ie. mail-ins).
  • Share the information they found at this site with friends who need convincing.
  • Encourage people to donate to the organization.

Audience

  • Educate themselves on this issue.
  • Share site with those who need this information (DRUG WAR CLOCK)
  • Donate to support the cause.
  • Be involved in local government voting processes.

Website

  • Crystal clear navigation to information and actions.
  • Provide a source for people to come back to repeatedly, with updated news articles on the issue.
  • Provide a very simple and easy to use petition / mail in system for those who want to take action.

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Young People, developing new ideas Learn more, share info across platforms. Get involved in local government, mail in petitions.
Middle Class Families Learn more, share info across platforms. Donate to the org, spread info to friends.
Local Government Officials To listen to the feedback provided with this website. To make a change and cut hard approaches to the war on drugs.
Formally Addicted Spread awareness of the issue first hand, write about experiences, help with first hand accounts and better sources of the trauma this system induces. Publish an account on their story (personal stories sell empathy well).
Conservatives provide easily accessible, well cited articles to browse, provide shock factor information such as the drug war clock. Change of trained thinking through these methods. (No physical action is expected from this demographic)

Conclusions

This was very helpful for figuring out what audience was for this website. With the data above it can be determined that Young Adults are the primary target audience with the primary objective being to inform them about drug policy issues. The goal needs to be easy to access, non-commital options to help Young People better understand the issue at hand, while still being able to cater to those who want to put money forward. This was further concluded by Interviews which were happening at this time as well.
